
Cumlative data (total time spent between a pair of people) is from
http://www.sociopatterns.org/datasets/infectious-sociopatterns/
(This is for the Dublin Art Gallery dataset).

Time series data ("listcontacts") is from Ayman Shalaby's thesis
(provided by his advisor Dan Lizotte).
data.csv is the processed version.
Each row has the form
 person1 person2 time
which records the time stamp at which they interacted
